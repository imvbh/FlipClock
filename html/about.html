FlipClock

About

This FlipClock application serves as a versatile and efficient tool suitable for various purposes, such as studying or as a screensaver. Notably, it operates without the need for an internet connection and boasts minimal latency, ensuring accurate timekeeping with perfect updates.

Key Features

  • Smooth Flips: The clock animations are remarkably smooth, setting this FlipClock apart from others available on the internet.
  • Full Screen: A convenient double-tap feature allows you to switch to full-screen mode for enhanced visibility and focus.
  • Time Formats: You can effortlessly switch between 12-hour and 24-hour time formats, adapting to your preference and needs.
  • Seconds Toggle: The option to enable or disable seconds provides flexibility in displaying time information.
  • Day/Night Mode: Enjoy a comfortable viewing experience with the ability to switch between day and night modes, ensuring readability in various lighting conditions.
  • Auto-Hide Buttons: To maximize your clock's visual appeal and minimize distractions, all control buttons automatically disappear after 2 seconds of user inactivity.
